# Center Hit Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

## English

`Center Hit` respects your privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and what third-party services may process information when you use the app on iOS or Android.

### 1. Data We Store

`Center Hit` stores gameplay data locally on your device. This may include:

- game progress
- unlocked packs and levels
- stars and best scores
- last played level
- app settings such as sound, music, vibration, and language preferences

This local data is used only to make the game work correctly on your device.

### 2. Accounts and Cloud Data

`Center Hit` does not require account creation.

At the time of this policy:

- no login or registration is required
- no profile system is used
- no cloud sync is provided by the app

### 3. Advertising

`Center Hit` may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect or process device and usage information to provide, improve, measure, and personalize ads, subject to Google's own policies and your device/platform settings.

For more information, see:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

### 4. Analytics

At the time of this policy, `Center Hit` does not use a dedicated in-app analytics service for player accounts or behavior tracking beyond what may be processed by platform services or advertising providers.

### 5. Purchases

At the time of this policy, `Center Hit` does not provide user accounts or subscription-based cloud services. If in-app purchases are added in the future, this policy may be updated.

### 6. Children's Privacy

`Center Hit` is designed for a broad audience, but it is not specifically directed to children under the age threshold defined by applicable law in your region.

Parents or guardians who believe that personal information has been improperly handled may contact us using the details below.

### 7. Data Retention and Deletion

Because most game data is stored locally on your device, you can remove much of that data by:

- using the in-app reset progress feature
- deleting the app from your device

Please note that advertising providers may process certain data independently under their own policies.

### 8. Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have privacy rights regarding personal data processed by third parties such as advertising providers. Please refer to the relevant provider policies and your platform privacy settings for more information.

### 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted at this page with a revised effective date.

### 10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

- Email: `support@centerhit.app`

You should replace this email with your real support address before release.
